===============
Instructions
===============

You are the orange fish.

- Swipe to move the fish
- Tap or Key press over the leap in order to "stop" the fish.

- Left Clicking with the mouse will reset the game.

You can kill smaller fish by swimming over them.  You will grow larger for ever smaller fish you consume.  Attempting to eat larger fish will result in a game over.

================
Known Bugs
================
- When you grow too large, you can't move your fish anymore.  Just left-click with the mouse to reset.
